Glasgow's journey to becoming the vibrant city we know today took a major step in 1175 when it was granted the status of a burgh. Meaning that 2025 marks Glasgow's 850th birthday, and we bottled some whisky celebrate!

It's a huge milestone and everyone who has or does call Glasgow home is invited to the party.

We're bottling 170 of each of the 5 labels in this collection. So get yours and mark the celebration!

Part of the Collaborations collection.

  • Tasting notes

    Appearance: pale straw colour with fat and fast forming legs! Nose: Wet cut grass and spicy liquorice, with a wee hint of lime juice. Palate: Lush!!! Light and mouth-watering green fruit flavours like granny smith apples, pear drops, and fresh squeezed lime juice. Finish: warming but short on intensity, though the soft citric fruit flavour lingers pleasantly long!
